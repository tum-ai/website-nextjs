Embed Events
Have your own site? Embed your calendar to easily share a live view of your upcoming events.
Paste the following HTML code snippet to your page:

<iframe
  src="https://lu.ma/embed/calendar/cal-GyF9L9O3ULx9C66/events"
  width="600"
  height="450"
  frameborder="0"
  style="border: 1px solid #bfcbda88; border-radius: 4px;"
  allowfullscreen=""
  aria-hidden="false"
  tabindex="0"
></iframe>


Appearance
By default, the widget uses the system appearance of the browser. If you would like to force a light or dark appearance, you can append lt=light or lt=dark to the end of the URL, like:

<iframe
  src="https://lu.ma/embed/calendar/cal-GyF9L9O3ULx9C66/events?lt=light"
  ...other attributes
></iframe>
